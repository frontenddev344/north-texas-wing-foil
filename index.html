2026 Wing Foil Race Dates- Saturday, April 18th! Join us at our annual N Texas Wing Foil Regatta at Rockledge Park on Grapevine Lake. All ages and skills, all foilers welcome. Featuring Wing Foil, E-Foil and Dockstarting.

Wing Foil Race Info

Strictly for beginners to the sport. Beginner/ basic course with 1 turn down and back. All beginners will need to wear a helmet and comp jacket.

Depending on the wind direction, the distance race is approximately 5 miles in total length. All skill levels welcome to participate here.

Technical race is a Slalom course with multiple buoy turns. You must know how to jibe in order to participate in this race. Competitors will have an on water jet ski start. If you are a Technical competitor you will be able to compete in at least 2 heats. The first race will consist of all competitors and the top 4 competitors will advance to the Finals while the remaining competitors are able to race again in Heat 2 and then the top 4 competitors from Heat 2 will advance to the Finals.
